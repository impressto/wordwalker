Privacy Policy for WordWalker

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Introduction

WordWalker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Spanish vocabulary learning application.

Information We Collect

WordWalker is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your game progress (points, streak count)
  • Questions you've already answered
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

No Third-Party Services

WordWalker does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect it.

No Account Required

You can use WordWalker without creating an account or providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

WordWalker is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, we comply with children's privacy protection regulations including COPPA.

Data Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage mechanisms. The security of this data depends on your device's security settings.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can clear all app data by clearing your browser's local storage
  • You can uninstall the app at any time
  • All data will be removed when you clear app data or uninstall

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
