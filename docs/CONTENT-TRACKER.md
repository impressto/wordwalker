## ğŸ“‹ Question Design Principles

All questions follow these guidelines:

### Structure
- **Spanish Question:** Clear, natural phrasing
- **English Translation:** Accurate but slightly awkward to encourage Spanish thinking
- **Options:** 3-4 plausible wrong answers + 1 correct answer
- **Emoji:** Visual aid for context/category
- **Difficulty:** easy, medium, or hard
- **Points:** Based on difficulty (5-15 points)
- **Hint:** Context-based clue (not the direct answer)

### Quality Standards
- âœ… Questions test vocabulary, not reading comprehension
